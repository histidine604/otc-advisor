Using kilograms (kg)
Label-standard ranges:
  • Acetaminophen: 10–15 mg/kg q4–6h (max 75 mg/kg/day; adult OTC max 3,000–4,000 mg/day depending on label/health).
  • Ibuprofen (≥6 months): 5–10 mg/kg q6–8h (max 40 mg/kg/day; adult OTC max 1,200 mg/day).
Acetaminophen
Common liquids: 160 mg/5 mL. Tablets: 325 mg, 500 mg.
Ibuprofen
Common liquids: 100 mg/5 mL. Tablets: 200 mg.
Safety notes: Avoid duplicate acetaminophen products; avoid ibuprofen in infants <6 months, late pregnancy, dehydration, ulcer/renal risk.
